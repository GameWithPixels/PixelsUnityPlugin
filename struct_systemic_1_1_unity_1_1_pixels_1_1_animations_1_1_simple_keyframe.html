Detailed Description

Stores a single keyframe of an LED animation. The keyframe is made of a time and an intensity. Size: 2 bytes, split this way:

  • 9 bits: time 0 - 511 in 500th of a second (i.e )
    • 1 -> 0.002s
    • 500 -> 1s
  • 7 bits: intensity (0 - 127)
